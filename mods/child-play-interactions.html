Child Play Interactions

Summary

Allows Child Sims to Fake Cry while standing or Draw Air Shapes while sitting at a table, for some extra silly fun and variety in their existence.

Features

  • Interactions

Interactions

With this mod, Child Sims will be able to:

  • Fake Cry: Does a crying animation while standing. It builds a little Fun and a little Creativity skill while it's running.
  • Draw Air Shapes: Requires a table to sit at. Your sim uses a crayon to draw shapes seemingly in mid-air. It builds a little Fun and a little Creativity skill while running.
