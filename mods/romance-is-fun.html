Romance is Fun for Romance Lovers

Not much to say about this mod that isn't stated in the summary. Sims who have the Romantic and/or Alluring trait will gain Fun upon doing successful Romantic interactions with another Sim.

That's it. I made this mod because I've always found it weird that Romantics, in particular, don't gain Fun from Romantic socialization.
