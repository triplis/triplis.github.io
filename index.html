Love and Hate

There's a lot of talk in the community about sims and their personalities feeling the same, and I've long wanted to do a mod project that would aim at addressing some aspect of this issue. Along those lines, I'm excited to introduce the new Love and Hate mod. This mod gives you a lot of power over what the sims in your world Love and Hate, affecting their autonomous behavior and giving them various bonuses or penalties for doing what they Love or Hate.

You can go get it now, below, on the new Love and Hate mod page and read all about the details of how it works.

Want to know when updates come out? Follow me on Patreon or join my discord server Triplis Station

Become a Sorcerer 3.0: Specializations

Investigate a Suspicious Lump of Clay to get infused with magic, choose a ritual to specialize, then use Specialization spells, focus on an alignment, and unlock more spells! Become a master of Specializations, along with mastery of the Black Arts or White Arts and specialize further with a ritual of your choice. Or level Mischief Arts Skill for a more neutral focus!

Love and Hate

Allows you to customize what a sim Loves and Hates from a variety of options, impacting autonomous behavior and providing bonuses or penalties when doing what one Loves or Hates.

Habitual Autonomous Behavior Instructions for Typical Morning (HABIT)

Allows you to customize a sim's waking routine, including order and actions. Possible priorities are: Bladder, Hygiene, Brush Teeth, Hunger, and Do Homework.

Lock Skill Gain Progress

Adds a pie menu called Skill Lock that enables you to Lock or Unlock skill gain for individuals Sims at will. An important addition for making Sims feel like individuals, rather than slowly becoming amazing at everything.

Literally a Door Portal

Adds a new door object called Portal Door Plain. With two or more placed, sims can path through them (regardless of distance, floors, etc.) to get where they need to be faster.

Thinking Skill

Level the new Thinking Skill, choose the new Philosopher Trait, and pursue two new Thinking-based aspirations!

8 Pack of Teen Exclusive Traits

8 unique traits for Teen Sims to expand your options with them, providing some extremes of personality to choose from.

8 Pack of Child Exclusive Traits

8 unique traits for Child Sims to expand your options with them, providing some extremes of personality to choose from.

Lot Trait Mods

Adds lot trait Celebrity Hideout, which automatically disguises NPC celebrities who show up on the lot.

Child and Teen can Quit or Rejoin School

Child and Teen Sims can choose to Quit or Join school at your whim. Comes with an optional UnlimitedPTO add-on (explained in detail on mod page).

Block Phone Dialog While Silenced

Normally, the "silence" feature on the phone will block the ringing when you get a phone notification, but not much else. This mod blocks the notifications entirely when the phone is silenced.

Phone Animation Replaced with Listening Animation

Wanting to play a time period before Phones, or just tired of seeing your Sims on them all the time? This mod replaces Phone animations with the listening animation.

Nap on the Ground

Can click on yourself and Nap or Sleep. Works like other Nap/Sleep, except you can do it anywhere and just Nap/Sleep on the ground. Teen or older can use it.

Plant Sim Interactions

Allows Plant Sims to absorb puddles, use a tranquilizing poison kiss, and discuss some plant things with other Plant Sims plantily.

Toddler Walk Together

Toddlers can walk together with other sims, leading them around, as they wander about with their little toddler legs, getting exercise and building their movement skill.

Toddler Potty Gloat

Toddlers with max Potty skill can click on a Potty and gloat about their dominion over it.

Child Play Interactions

Allows Child Sims to Fake Cry while standing or Draw Air Shapes while sitting at a table, for some extra silly fun and variety in their existence.

Music Lover Idle Animation in Live Mode

Sims who have the Music Lover trait will, while in Live Mode, occasionally do the same idle animation that plays when you click on the Music Lover trait in CAS.

Romance Gives Fun to Romantic and Alluring Trait Sims

Sims who have the Romantic and/or Alluring trait will gain Fun upon doing successful Romantic interactions with another Sim.

Social Pariah Trait

The hardcore, no-socializing trait. Must be added to Sims manually with cheats.

Walk Your Way

Provides toggle options when you click on your Sim to force their walkstyle to be Walk, Jog, or Run.
