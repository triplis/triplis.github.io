Donate

I have set up two different ways to financially support what I do. The one I have mainly used in the past is Patreon, where you pledge an amount you want to on a regular, monthly basis. I've now added a Paypal link, too, for those who want to support what I do more selectively, on an irregular basis. This is to account for the change in my time investment in modding. You can read about that here.

All donations are appreciated, regardless of the amount.

Patreon

You can donate on a monthly basis to my Patreon.

Paypal

You can donate more selectively, to my Paypal. For example, if you want to donate "per creation," meaning a donation for each mod or feature I make that you consider enough to be worth a donation, you can do that.
